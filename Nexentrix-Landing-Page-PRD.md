# Nexentrix Ltd Landing Page - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Overview

- Product Name: Nexentrix Ltd Landing Page
- Purpose:
  - Create a high-converting landing page
  - Explain what Nexentrix Ltd is
  - Showcase products: Lumina AI, Nexentrix Academy, Top-Up Platform
  - Attract users, partners, and investors
  - Capture leads (emails, sign-ups)

---

## 2. Goals & Objectives

### Primary Goals
- Build brand credibility
- Convert visitors into users or leads
- Clearly communicate company vision

### Secondary Goals
- Recruit talent
- Promote Nexentrix Academy
- Drive early adoption of products

---

## 3. Target Audience
- Students interested in AI (Africa-focused)
- Startups and businesses needing automation
- Tech enthusiasts
- Investors and partners

---

## 4. Page Structure (Sections)

### 4.1 Hero Section (Above the Fold)
- Headline: "Building the Future with AI"
- Subheadline: "AI-powered solutions, education, and automation for Africa and beyond."
- CTA Buttons: "Get Started", "Join Nexentrix Academy"
- Background: AI-themed animation or gradient (dark + neon style)

### 4.2 About Nexentrix Ltd
- Short explanation:
  - What Nexentrix is
  - Vision and mission
- Example copy: "Nexentrix Ltd is an AI-driven technology company building intelligent solutions, empowering individuals through education, and helping businesses automate and scale."

### 4.3 Our Divisions
- Cards grid (4 columns):
  - AI SaaS
  - Nexentrix Academy
  - Automation & Consulting
  - AI Influencing
- Each card includes short description

### 4.4 Products & Projects
- Showcase 3 items:
  1. Lumina AI (Healthcare AI, Predictive diagnostics)
  2. Nexentrix Academy (AI learning platform)
  3. Nexentrix Top-Up Platform (Airtime, bills, subscriptions)
- Each with icon/image, short description, "Learn More" button

### 4.5 Why Choose Us
- Bullet points:
  - AI-first approach
  - Built for Africa
  - Scalable technology
  - Real-world impact

### 4.6 Nexentrix Academy Highlight
- Focus on education
- Features:
  - AI courses
  - Live webinars
  - Community
  - Mentorship
- CTA: "Start Learning"

### 4.7 Call-To-Action Section
- Headline: "Join the Future of AI"
- Buttons: "Join Academy", "Partner With Us", "Contact Us"

### 4.8 Footer
- Company name
- Social links
- Contact email
- Navigation links

## 5. Design Requirements
- Style: Modern, futuristic, clean
- Color theme: Dark mode primary
- Colors: Black / Deep Blue, Purple / Neon Blue, White text
- Fonts: Headings bold modern (Poppins / Inter), body clean readable font

---

## 6. Functional Requirements
- Fully responsive (mobile + desktop)
- Fast loading speed
- Smooth scrolling
- Button hover animations
- Contact form (basic)

---

## 7. Tech Stack (Recommended)
- Frontend: React.js (or Next.js)
- Styling: Tailwind CSS
- Animations: Framer Motion
- Hosting: Vercel / Netlify
